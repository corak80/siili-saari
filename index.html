About Us

Where healing meets nature

Siili-Saari Care Center is a hedgehog rehabilitation center operated by Eläinklinikka Saari in Vaasa, Finland. We rescue, treat, and rehabilitate injured and orphaned hedgehogs (Erinaceus europaeus) before releasing them back into the wild.

The European hedgehog population faces growing threats from habitat loss, traffic, pesticides, and climate change. Our mission is to provide professional veterinary care for these beloved creatures and educate the community on how to coexist with them.

What We Do

From rescue to release

Rescue & First Aid

Emergency intake and stabilization for injured, sick, or orphaned hedgehogs found by the public. Immediate triage, warmth, and hydration.

Medical Treatment

Full veterinary diagnostics and care — from X-rays and surgery to parasite treatment, wound management, and medication protocols.

Rehabilitation & Release

Gradual recovery in natural-style outdoor enclosures. Weight monitoring, foraging skills assessment, and safe release into suitable habitats.

Education

Community outreach, school visits, and public awareness campaigns about hedgehog conservation and how to create hedgehog-friendly gardens.

How to Help

Found an injured hedgehog?

Every minute counts. Here's what to do while you wait for professional help.

01

Pick it up gently

Use gardening gloves or a thick towel. Hedgehogs can curl into a ball — this is normal, handle with care.

02

Place in a box

Use a cardboard box with air holes. Line the bottom with a towel or newspaper for comfort.

03

Keep warm & quiet

Place a warm water bottle wrapped in a towel beside (not on) the hedgehog. Keep in a quiet, dark room.

04

Call us immediately

Contact Siili-Saari Care Center. We'll guide you through next steps and arrange pickup if needed.
